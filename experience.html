Projects Summary

  1. Data Analysis in Tourists Destinations. Based on the big data of the tourists in Beijing, analyzing the preference of tourists, and formed a multi-dimensional visitor’s analysis report for government decision-making to provide reliable support with statistical analysis.

  2. A Telecommunications Company Customer Analysis. In order to boost sales, I was analyzing and modeling cellphone customers and call records, and found out the top ten percent of important customers, helping the marketing team to identify customers.

  3. Education and Training Industry Promotion with Baidu Advertisement. In order to save the cost on advertisement, categorized these users and sent them pop-up ads according to their preference and requirements, thus developing potential customers.

  4. Analysis of User Behaviors on A Website. Based on users’ access history, we identify potential targets of advertisements and provide suggestions to the runner of the website.

  5. Data Analysis and Commercial Value Based on the Data of Logistics Vehicle Networking. Based on the car-network data and vehicle insurance data, graded the driving behavior of each vehicle by multi-dimensional risk rating, depicted each vehicle, and provided the improvement suggestions for driving behavior of each vehicle. And at last it will bring commercial value to insurance companies, drivers and automobile manufacturers.
